Re-testing

Most accurate way to find your threshold pace. Repeat every 4–6 weeks — your zones update automatically when you do.

  1. Warm up for 10–15 minutes with a few short builds at the end.
  2. Paddle as hard as you can sustain for exactly 3 minutes. Pace it so you finish empty — not so hot you fade in the first minute.
  3. Note the average pace (per km or per mile) over those 3 minutes. That's your threshold pace — punch it in above.
  4. Cool down easy for 10 minutes.
