Common questions

Good to know.

Do I need to know my threshold pace to start?

It helps. The fastest way to find it is a 3-minute test — paddle as hard as you can sustain for three continuous minutes, and use the resulting pace as your threshold. Our pace calculator then gives you every zone target.

How many sessions per week?

Two structured sessions — typically one interval session and one longer distance session. You can add your own easy paddling around that if your schedule allows.

What happens after the 4 weeks?

Your subscription renews for another 4-week block at the next progression level. You retest your threshold, your zones update, and the sessions adjust accordingly. Or cancel — no questions asked.

Can I switch disciplines later?

Yes. You can switch between Prone, SUP, Outrigger Canoe, and Surf Ski from your member settings. The 4-week cycle stays the same.

Why pick Progressive over Custom?

If you don't have a goal race you're building toward, a Progressive block gives you real structure without the commitment. You can always move up to the Custom Plan later when a race is on the calendar.
